Gordon Kean Clarke

Gordon Kean Clarke has spent three decades investigating the intersection of documented anomaly and unexplained phenomenon — from the Bermuda Triangle to near-death experiences, cryptids, UFO testimony, and paranormal case studies. His approach is methodological rather than credulous: he applies the same evidentiary standards to extraordinary claims that a good journalist would apply to any story, which is why his conclusions carry genuine weight.
